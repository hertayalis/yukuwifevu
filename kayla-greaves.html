Kayla Greaves - InStyle

Title: Executive Beauty Editor

Education: McMaster University, Humber College

Location: New York, NY

Expertise: Hair, Skincare, Wellness

  • Kayla Greaves is the Executive Beauty Editor for InStyle, overseeing all beauty coverage on the site.
  • She has previously held positions at HuffPost and Bustle.

Experience

Kayla Greaves is the Executive Beauty Editor of InStyle. She has previously held positions of HuffPost and Bustle. Her work has appeared in BuzzFeed, Teen Vogue, ELLE, and more. In 2017, she was named on the "Top 100 Black Women to Watch in Canada" list and appeared on HBO's The Beauty of Blackness documentary in 2021.

Education

Kayla received a Bachelor of Arts in Communication Studies from McMaster University, as well as a post-graduate degree in Public Relations at Humber College

About InStyle

InStyle is an award-winning destination for fashion, beauty, and pop-culture, with an all-are-welcome ethos that swings open the proverbial velvet ropes. Whether you’re looking for a wardrobe refresh, haircut inspiration, or a story that’ll make sparkling cocktail-party conversation, our expert-connected coverage is where to start.
