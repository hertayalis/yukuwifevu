Jada Pinkett Smith breaks silence after husband Will Smith slapped Chris Rock at the Oscars



Jada Pinkett Smith is speaking out after her husband Will Smith hit Chris Rock onstage at the 94th annual Academy Awards.

The actress took to Instagram to write: “This is a season for healing and I’m here for it.”

She restricted her comment section after sharing the post.

This comes after her husband Will Smith apologised to Chris Rock for slapping him over a joke he made about Jada Pinkett Smith’s hair.
