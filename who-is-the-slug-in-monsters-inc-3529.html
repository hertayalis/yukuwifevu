What is a surfing snail?

Agaronia propatula is a sea snail found on main beaches on the Pacific coast of Central America. Although generally slow moving, these snails will extend their foot (“pseudopod”) to form a sail and help them catch small waves. In doing so, they can move rapidly along the beach.
