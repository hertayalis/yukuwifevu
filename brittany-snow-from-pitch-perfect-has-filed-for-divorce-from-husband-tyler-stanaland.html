Brittany Snow, from Pitch Perfect, has filed for divorce from husband Tyler Stanaland


The star of ‘Pitch Perfect’, Brittany Snow, has officially filed for divorce from her husband, Tyler Stanaland, a reality TV personality.

TMZ reported that the divorce filing took place on Wednesday, January 18, following nearly three years of marriage.

Irreconcilable differences have been cited as the reason for the split, with Snow confirming the existence of a prenuptial agreement.

In September 2022, the couple publicly announced their separation. On the same day, Tyler was seen being affectionate with his “Selling the OC” co-star, Alex Hall, although he insisted they were just friends.

Brittany and Tyler got married on March 14, 2020, during the onset of the pandemic. The couple does not have any children together.
