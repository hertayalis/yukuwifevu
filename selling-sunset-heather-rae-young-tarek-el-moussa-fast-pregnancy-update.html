Selling Sunset's Heather Rae Young offers fertility update two months after wedding

Heather is step-mom to two children

    Selling Sunset star Heather Rae Young, who recently wed Tarek El-Moussa, has shared that her fertility journey is happening "faster" than expected.

    EXCLUSIVE: Mary Fitzgerald talks feud with Christine, Heather's wedding and Chrishell's break-up

    The 34 year old took to Instagram to share the news with fans that she has already shared injections to "stimulate follicles" as the pair begin the process of considering having children. "Just left my fertility doctor. Things are happening faster than I thought. I'm actually starting my injections tonight to stimulate my follicles and my eggs to get me ready for the retrieval, which should be happening in about two weeks," she shared in a video to fans.

    WATCH: Selling Sunset season 3 trailer

    The realtor added that she would be injecting "two different shots tonight and every day for the next week and a half", and although she admitted that she may not show that side of the treatment because it's a "little invasive" she would be happy to "talk through the process".

    She already has six eggs "on ice" but previously shared that she would be having her blood work checked to keep an eye on levels.

    MORE: Inside the Selling Sunset stars' weddings: Christine Quinn, Mary Fitzgerald and Crishell Stause

    MORE: Selling Sunset season four: Everything we know so far

    The reality star had noted that "in the past, I had low fertility and low egg count" and so she is freezing her embryos to prepare for all eventualities.

    Tarek, 40, is already a dad to two children - daughter Taylor, 11, and six-year-old son Brayden - whom he welcomed with ex-wife Christina Haack.

    Heather took to Insta to share the journey with fans

    However, although Heather has opened up in the past about her decision not to have children, in November Tarek shared that spending time with his children had changed her mind.

    "Before, she was never around kids, and now she's raising Taylor and Braydon with me," he revealed.

    "And we have them [half the time] so she's full-on 'mom life,' and she's realizing she can do it. And she loves it."

    The pair wed on 23 October in front of 150 guests in Montecito, California.

    The blended family recited vows to each other at Tarek and Heather's wedding

    Heather told magazine that she couldn't stop crying in the moments before she walked down the aisle, bawling in her father's arms because she was "more nervous than I've ever been in my life".

    "Walking down the aisle with my dad was always a dream of mine," Heather added.

    "I hate that I'm saying this, but I wanted him to see me walk down the aisle and become a wife before he passes away. It was a very special moment to me, because I never knew I was going to find my soulmate.

    "And I know my parents want nothing more than for me to be with a man that protects me and loves me like Tarek does. Having my dad walk me down the aisle and hand me off to my husband was everything."
