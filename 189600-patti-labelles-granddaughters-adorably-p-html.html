Patti LaBelle's Granddaughters Gia and Leyla Adorably Pose in Matching Blue Swimsuits in New Photos

Patti LaBelle's grandkids, Gia and Leyla Edwards, storms Instagram with charming appearances in photos featuring them and their mom.

The Instagram post saw two of Patti's granddaughters looking smashing in blue colored outfits. Their mom was not left out of the equation as she donned a blue-colored flowing dress.

The singer's older granddaughter and daughter-in-law Lona Edwards, who is married to Zuri Edwards, Patti's only son, also crowned their twinning look with rocking sunglasses.

Patti LaBelle performing at the 2017 New Orleans Jazz & Heritage Festival. | Photo: Getty Images

The little princesses may seem smashing and harmless; however, their grandma revealed that they make fun of her whenever they choose to, which she doesn't mind. She said:

"I let them make a fool of me ok holidays, any day. Anytime they come to my house; they take my jewelry, they break things…"

She continued by informing People via an interview that she has the best times with the sisters and never worry about what they do.

Apart from being a playful grandma, Patti is also an excellent chef who likes to cook her meals even when she's hosting a crowd. For the holiday season, she mentioned that she hosts over 100 people and loves to work on the menu herself without anyone's assistance.

She stated during an interview that her two grandbabies love to dance and sing and that she's sure they will have a career in the entertainment industry later on.

When Patti is not talking about her family, then she's flaunting them online. Ever since she welcomed her first grandkid, Gia, she regularly uses the gram to show off the adorable cutie and mother.

Furthermore, at just one-year-old, her oldest grandchild has been dressed in adorable, eye-catching outfits. Grandma Patti uploaded an image showing her in jeans, shorts, gladiator sandals, and a black hat. She wrote:

"My stylish little mini diva, Gia, is just too cute and fierce."

The award-winning entertainer also engages in cheerful activities with the girls. She revealed that both Gia and Leyla love to sing, and they all sing along to various rhymes all the time. It appears the apple doesn't fall far from the tree after all.

When they are not singing in the house, then they are singing on stage alongside their grandma.
