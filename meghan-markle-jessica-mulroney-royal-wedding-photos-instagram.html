Meghan Markle's Best Friend Jessica Mulroney Displays Royal Wedding Photos at Home

The Duchess of Sussex's BFF, Jessica Mulroney, took to her Instagram Stories this weekend to give fans a little insight into how her family celebrates the festive season. And as reported by Hello! magazine, Instagram user @_duchess_of_sussex spotted that Mulroney has some sweet photos from the royal wedding displayed in her house.

Understandably, Mulroney has royal wedding photos involving her children framed, as her daughter and her twin sons were in Meghan's royal bridal party. One is a photo of Mulroney and her daughter Ivy on the steps of St. George's Chapel in Windsor Castle, right before they entered for the royal wedding. The other photo is one of the iconic shots taken of Meghan's dramatic wedding veil, which Mulroney's twin boys, John and Brian, held as the bride ascended the chapel steps.

Mulroney was, of course, very much involved in the royal wedding, with royal commentator Omid Scobie revealing back in January 2018 that, "BFF and bridal expert Jessica Mulroney is guiding Meghan though the process and quietly flew in to London on Jan 10, spending four days at the couple’s Nottingham Cottage home."

Clearly, Mulroney wants to remember Meghan and Harry's royal wedding for a very long time to come.
