Who is Ted Danson?

Ted Danson birthed as Edward Bridge Danson III, commonly known as Ted Danson, was born on December 29, 1947, in San Diego, California. He and his older sister Jan were reared in Flagstaff, Arizona, where their father was an archaeologist and the director of the Museum of Northern Arizona from 1959 until 1975. He went to the Kent School, a Connecticut prep school. He was a star basketball player there. He completed his study at Stanford University, where he developed an interest in acting. In search of a better acting program, he relocated to Carnegie Mellon University, where he received his Bachelor of Fine Arts in drama in 1972.
