Sports Star Relationships With Uncomfortable Age Gaps

Sheldon Souray and Tess Annique's love story begins in an unlikely place. As the model and wellness influencer told Brides, both she and the NHL All-Star were battling demons when they first crossed paths in 2017. "Our love story is unique because we met in treatment," she shared. "We were both in a very low place when we met." They eventually left treatment and lost touch but reconnected six months later. The Montreal Canadiens alum invited Annique to visit him in Idaho that Labor Day, and she accepted. "Fast forward four years, and we got married the exact weekend we reconnected," she told the mag, gushing, "We've been able to inspire each other to become the best versions of ourselves, support one another's sobriety, and live full, authentic lives."

Indeed, having that shared experience has created an unbreakable bond. "He was my ride or die best friend just because we had both gone through such intense stuff," she told the "Neon Confidential" podcast. And despite Souray being 17 years older, they continue to inspire one another. Celebrating three years of sobriety in 2020, Annique praised her partner on Instagram, writing, "Thank you for being my human: my greatest teacher, my greatest friend, my greatest blessing." Meanwhile, in 2022, he celebrated her birthday by gushing, "You've picked me up and dusted me off and let me share in the strength and courage of your journey." 

Jump to July 2023, and the couple announced they were expecting their first child.
