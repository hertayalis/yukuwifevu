Anna Heinrich - Age, Family, Bio

Reality star best known for winning the first season of The Bachelor Australia.

Before Fame

She worked as a lawyer, earning her degree from the University of Technology Sydney and earning her bachelors in anthropology and gender studies from Sydney University.

Trivia

After her Bachelor win, she launched her LoveAlwaysAnna beauty and lifestyle blog.

Family Life

As the winner of the Bachelor Australia, she began dating Tim Robards in 2013. They got engaged in 2017 and married in 2018. They have a daughter named Ellie. They are currently expecting their second child together. She has an older sister named Andrea.

Associated With

The Bachelor Australia later starred Blake Garvey.
