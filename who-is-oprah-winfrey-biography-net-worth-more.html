Who is Oprah Winfrey: Biography, Net Worth &amp; More

Who is Oprah Winfrey: Biography, Net Worth & More- Oprah Winfrey, an American aged 69 is an American talk show host, television producer, actress, author, and media proprietor widely known for her talk show, The Oprah Winfrey Show, broadcast from Chicago.

Who is Oprah Winfrey?

The daughter of Vernita Lee, a housemaid, and Vernon Winfrey, a coal miner, Oprah Gail Winfrey was born on January 29, 1954, in Kosciusko, Mississippi in the United States of America. The ace television personality spent most of her earliest years living with her grandmother in rural Mississippi. She was disliked by most of the kids in her school and found her main joy in learning and reading. She also had a knack and joy for speaking, especially public speaking.

At the age of six, she went to live with her mother in an apartment in Milwaukee. While there, she received hardly any attention from her mother, who was busy with work, a new daughter, and a boyfriend. Oprah moved to Nashville to live with her father, but after less than a year, she moved back in with her mother.

At the age of nine, Oprah was raped by an older cousin, and for the next several years, she was sexually abused by several other male relatives and friends of her mother but remained silent about it. Oprah also became very rebellious at that time, and her mother felt that she couldn’t handle her anymore.

At age 14, she was sent back to Nashville and lived with her father and his new wife. They quickly became a very positive influence on her.

What is Oprah Winfrey’s age, height, and weight?

Presently Oprah who was born on January 29, 1954, is 69 years old. She stands at a height of 5 ft 6 inches or 169 cm (1.69 m) tall, and her weight is only 77 kg or 170 pounds.

What is Oprah Winfrey’s Nationality and Ethnicity?

Oprah is an American national and belongs to an Afro-American ethnic background.

What is Oprah Winfrey’s profession?

Oprah was in high school, she began her broadcasting career as a newswoman for a Nashville radio station. In 1971, she entered Tennessee State University, and shortly later became a television news anchor for a Nashville station.

In 1976, Oprah went to Baltimore and briefly worked as a reporter and co-anchor for their ABC affiliate. However, she found that objective reporting was not her style, especially since she displayed so much emotion and personal involvement in her reporting.She soon moved on to become co-host of a local talk show called People Are Talking. The show was very successful, and Oprah found her fit as a talk show host.

In 1984, Oprah went to Chicago to host a struggling talk show called A.M. Chicago. She quickly turned the show into a phenomenon, and after less than one year it was renamed The Oprah Winfrey Show.By 1986, the show entered national syndication, and it has been the number-one talk show in America ever since. It currently draws over 20 million US viewers per week and is also broadcast in over 100 other countries.

Through her production company Harpo Productions, Oprah owns and produces The Oprah Winfrey Show (which she bought the rights to in 1988), and is one of the wealthiest people in the media industry.Among her other ventures, Oprah has had numerous television and movie roles, runs a magazine called O, is a co-founder of Oxygen Media, produces films for ABC, is involved in many philanthropic activities, and speaks actively against child abuse.

How many children does Oprah Winfrey have?

Oprah when she was 14 became pregnant and gave birth to a son prematurely, he died shortly after his birth.

How did Oprah become a billionaire?

After failed negotiations with her agent and King World/ABC, Oprah created HARPO Productions and produced for herself, opening the door to a billionaire empire.

What religion is Oprah Winfrey?

Oprah is a Christian and was raised in the Baptist church. She describes herself as a consistent reader of the Bible, and through her television show, basically built the church that she wanted.

What did Oprah Winfrey fight for?

She does all that she can to eradicate child abuse. As a victim herself, Winfrey knows the damage abuse does to young lives. She was a major force in the drafting, lobbying, and passage of the National Child Protection Act. The Act was signed into law by President Clinton in 1994.

Who is Oprah Winfrey married to?

She has dated several celebrities, including John Tesh and Roger Ebert, but has never married. She has officially been in a relationship with her long-time boyfriend Stedman Graham since 1986.
