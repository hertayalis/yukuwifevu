News on Chris Hemsworth's wife, Spanish model &amp; actress

Elsa Pataky, also known as Elsa Lafuente Medianu, is a Spanish model, actress and film producer well known for her role in the Fast & The Furious films where she played Elena Neves. The Madrid-born actress married fellow actor, Chris Hemsworth, in 2010 and the couple have since gone on to have three children together: daughter, India Rose, and twins, Sasha and Tristan.
