Pics: Alanna Panday - Ivor Wedding Tale

Article by Suman M Published on: 7:02 pm, 17 March 2023

Ananya Panday’s cousin Alanna Panday got married to her beau Ivor McCray in Mumbai on Thursday. The wedding pictures are now out.

Alanna posted the pictures and captioned the bunch with, ‘Yesterday was a fairy tale, I love you more than anything in the world @ivor can’t wait to start a family with you 🤍♾️’

Alanna Panday is the daughter of fitness instructor and author Deanne Panday and Chikki Pandey (Chunky Panday’s bother).

The wedding event had the bridegroom Ivor McCray dancing to RRR’s Naatu Naatu song and Ananya Panday performing for ‘Saat Samundar Paar’ along with her dad Chunky Panday.
