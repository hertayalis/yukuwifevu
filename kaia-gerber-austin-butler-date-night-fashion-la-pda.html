Kaia Gerber and Austin Butler's Date Night Is Filled With Fashion

Kaia Gerber and Austin Butler were spotted on a low-key date night in Los Angeles this weekend, and photos prove even the pair’s off-duty looks are insanely stylish.

In the images, the model and the actor are seen walking arm in arm through a parking lot in the evening—seemingly attempting to go incognito by wearing black face masks.

Gerber embraces the beloved coastal grandmother aesthetic in a breezy white slipdress with a light blue print that appears to be made up of tiny flower illustrations. (The photos are quite dark.) She complements the summery dress with the coveted black Adidas Samba sneakers, worn with long white scrunched-up socks, and a light green sweater, which she wears over her shoulders. She additionally accessorizes with brown oval sunglasses.

Butler looks cool in his usual Elvis Presley–esque style. He wears a white T-shirt with a light blue vintage-inspired graphic, loose black trousers, shiny brown leather shoes, a black Carhartt jacket, and a blue NASA trucker hat.

BACKGRID

The pair—who have been dating since late 2021—don’t post each other on social media and mostly stay out of the Hollywood spotlight, but are often seen supporting one another at events around the world while showing off their fashion sense.

Earlier this month, they were pictured wearing coordinating brown street looks while out in Paris for Fashion Week. They stopped at hotspot Costes—a hotel with a lively restaurant and bar. For the occasion, Gerber was sleek in a brown slipdress, over which she layered a long black coat. For footwear, she opted for tall black heeled boots, and she accessorized with a leather black shoulder bag, a long gold chain necklace, and gold hoop earrings. Elvis star Butler, meanwhile, looked effortlessly cool as ever in a plain white T paired with a pair of brown jeans and a cropped grayish-blue workwear-inspired collared jacket. He accessorized with a distressed brown leather belt with a silver buckle, a dainty silver chain necklace, and a vintage navy trucker hat from the annual Sturgis Motorcycle Rally.
