Roman Reigns Advertised For Handicap Match At Upcoming House Shows

Roman Reigns will team up with The Usos next week to take on Edge and Rey & Dominik Mysterio on SmackDown this week, but Roman will be outnumbered during outcoming house shows.

The Tribal Chief just beat Rey Mysterio in a Hell in a Cell match, but they are not done with their feud. The two will continue things when WWE hits the road for some upcoming Supershow events.

Roman Reigns is being advertised for a handicap match against Rey Mysterio and Dominik Mysterio at the upcoming Supershow event on July 24th in Pittsburgh. The card is always subject to change, but it appears that this match is on.

Reigns will defend the Universal Championship against Edge at Money in the Bank. He will then face John Cena at SummerSlam in match that is “locked in.” You can click here for a spoiler on when John Cena is expected back.

The SmackDown Tag Team Champions vs the Universal Champion seems like quite a draw for a house show. We’ll have to see if it brings in fans to see this unique match.
