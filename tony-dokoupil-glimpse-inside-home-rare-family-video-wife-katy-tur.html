Tony Dokoupil shares rare family video with wife from his impressive New York home

The two don't shy away from sharing their debates on social media

    Tony Dokoupil is proving his wife wrong! Or is he? The CBS star shared an adorable video of him and his wife bantering with one another from their impressive New York kitchen.

    MORE: Tony Dokoupil reveals rare glimpse of married life as he hilariously calls out his wife

    His wife, Katy Tur, is also a television journalist, reporting for MSNBC. They have been married since 2017, and have two children together, Teddy and Eloise, and Tony has two other children from a previous relationship.

    While the journalist only tends to share sweet captures of family life or sticks to hard-hitting news, his wife just shared a video of him like you've never seen him before.

    The video, shared to Katy's Instagram Stories, sees the news anchor in a much more casual manner than fans are used to seeing him, wearing flip flops, loose shorts, and a t-shirt.

    It catches him washing the dishes from their spacious kitchen on a farmhouse sink. The impressive space features green cabinets, a large wooden table from where the MSNBC reporter was recording her husband, tall windows, and open shelving.

    While the star was in fact washing the dishes, and most would appreciate the kind gesture, his wife wasn't totally convinced of her husband's intentions.
