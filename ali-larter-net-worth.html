Ali Larter Net Worth

Net Worth and Salary

Ali Larter’s net worth as of 2023 is estimated to be $12 million. Larter has amassed a fortune from her successful career in the entertainment industry. She has worked in a variety of movies and TV shows, which include popular titles such as ‘Final Destination,’ ‘Heroes,’ and ‘Resident Evil: Extinction.’ In addition, she has also appeared in numerous campaigns of popular brands like Estée Lauder, Gallo Family Vineyards, and numerous others.

Early Life and Education

Ali Larter was born on February 28, 1976, in Cherry Hill, New Jersey, USA. Her mother, Margaret Larter, was a real estate agent, while her father, Danforth Larter, was a trucking executive. Larter attended Carusi Middle School and Cherry Hill High School West before attending a drama school. She was highly interested in acting from a young age. After completing high school, Larter moved to Tokyo to pursue a modeling career.

Career

Larter began her acting career in 1997 with a role in the TV series ‘Suddenly Susan.’ Her acting skills and talent soon landed her opportunities for other projects. In 1999, she starred in the horror movie ‘House on Haunted Hill.’ In 2000, she played the role of Clear Rivers in the movie ‘Final Destination.’ She has also been a part of various successful movies like ‘Legally Blonde,’ ‘Jay and Silent Bob Strike Back,’ and ‘Obsessed.’ In addition, she played the role of Niki Sanders and her alter ego Jessica in the popular TV series ‘Heroes.’

Personal Life

Ali Larter is married to actor Hayes MacArthur since 2009. The couple has two children together, son Theodore and daughter Vivienne. Larter is also passionate about food and cooking and released her cookbook called ‘Kitchen Revelry’ in 2013.

Real Estate

Ali Larter’s lifestyle is quite luxurious and extravagant. She owns a beautiful house in California’s Brentwood area, which she bought for $2.9 million in 2013. Her home has beautiful interiors with a spacious manicured garden and a stunning view from the balcony.

Legacy

Ali Larter is a highly accomplished actress and has made great strides in the entertainment industry. Her legacy includes inspiring many women who are aspiring to make a career in acting. Her dedication to her craft, hard work, and charisma continue to make her a role model for many aspiring actors.
