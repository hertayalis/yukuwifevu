Watch Finn Wolfhard from 'Stranger Things' Cover Velvet Underground

In case you've somehow missed the memo, the kids of Stranger Things are insanely talented. Not only are they all talented actors, many of them have other hidden (and not-so-hidden) talents. Take Finn Wolfhard, for instance, who fronts the band Calpurnia in his free time.

Calpurnia performed Friday night at Brooklyn's Rough Trade NYC, playing a mix of covers and originals. Rolling Stone captured the group's cover of "Here She Comes Now" by Velvet Underground and it will blow you away.

"My dad and my mom used to play that kind of music in the car when I was a baby. And then my mom introduced me to the Beatles, and I got obsessed with the Beatles, and then I just went from there. I would also search the web: You know how one video leads to another leads to another? It was kind of like that, but with songs. A Beatles song led to a Led Zeppelin song, and then a Led Zeppelin song led to the Rolling Stones," Wolfhard said of what got him into music.
